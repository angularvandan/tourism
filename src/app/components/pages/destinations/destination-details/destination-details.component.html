Boracay
Island in Malay, Aklan, Philippines
Boracay, one of the top Islands in the Philippines, is popular for its beaches and blessed with a long stretch of powdery white sand, crystal clear and azure waters, and a stunning sunset. Boracay is the most iconic island in the Philipines and has won multiple awards in previous years.
Want to explore ?
Visiting Tips
For a unique Boracay experience, head to White Beach at dawn for a peaceful, crowd-free sunrise—perfect for photos without the usual hustle. Most visitors don’t realize you can book a local outrigger boat to explore hidden snorkeling spots beyond the tourist areas, where vibrant marine life flourishes. For a quieter evening, venture to the farthest end of Station 3, where the beachfront is more serene, and you can often find locals offering fresh, off-the-menu seafood dinners. Lastly, visit during the Amihan season (November to March) to catch impromptu beach yoga sessions or join locals in paddleboarding during calm afternoons.

Travel Tips
For a unique White Beach experience, avoid the midday rush by exploring nearby quieter spots like Diniwid Beach, just a short walk away. Instead of the typical sunset cruises, rent a paraw sailboat for a more intimate and traditional sunset view. Many tourists don't know that early mornings offer not just peaceful sunrises but also beach clean-ups with locals, a great way to give back. Also, explore the beach’s hidden eateries located behind beachfront resorts—they offer local dishes at lower prices. Lastly, pack a foldable beach mat, as loungers can get booked up quickly during peak hours.
Adventure Tips
For a truly unique adventure at White Beach, experience the thrill of a night snorkeling tour to see bioluminescent organisms light up the water, a rare sight that most tourists miss. Discover hidden, untouched coves accessible only by kayak, where you can find secret spots for snorkeling and relaxation away from the main crowds. For an adrenaline rush, try cliff diving from lesser-known rock formations along the northern coast, offering stunning views and a private jump experience. Engage with local surf instructors who offer off-the-beaten-path surf lessons at secluded breaks, ensuring a more personalized and exciting ride. Finally, explore an exclusive underwater photography tour led by local experts to capture rare marine species and unique underwater scenery not commonly documented.
